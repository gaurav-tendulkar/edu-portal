Activities â€” Design Innovation Centre (DIC), SPPU

Workshops, seminars, training sessions and events focused on innovation, entrepreneurship and industry collaboration.

The Design Innovation Centre (DIC) at Savitribai Phule Pune University runs a variety of workshops and events: "Innovative Entrepreneurship: Turning Ideas into Startups", "Design Thinking for Problem Solving", IPR and Patents workshops, Creative Writing & Content Creation, R&D for industry collaboration, Blockchain & AI sessions, Digital Marketing for Startups, Sustainable Development through Innovation, Product Prototyping and Networking for Career Success. These are aimed at students, teachers, researchers and industry participants to build skills and foster collaboration.
