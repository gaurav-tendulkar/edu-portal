I. Abhikalp: Design Competition for Innovative Projects

DIC-SPPU conducted ABHIKALP: A Design Competition for Innovative Projects & Proof of Concept Development during 2018 and 2019. Students presented their innovative project ideas before evaluation experts.

Around 20 winners from Abhikalp 2019 received financial assistance from DIC-SPPU to develop prototypes/products and explore potential for commercialization and IPR.
