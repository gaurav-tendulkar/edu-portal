WeWake IndiGreen Pvt. Ltd.

Bioplastics from agro-waste â€” compostable, recyclable and low carbon footprint alternatives to single-use plastics.

WeWake IndiGreen has developed the bioplastic from potential agro-waste. Different feedstock were tested and used for the production of bioplastic process and product development. The Team has developed different bioplastic products like flat sheets,
bags, cups, straws, ear-buds, beads etc. The results are optimistic which ensures that proper utilization of potential agro-waste for bioplastic production without leaving any carbon footprint on environment. Developed bioplastic results into promising alternative to single use oil based plastic which is currently a big havoc to environment. The developed bioplastic is to target packaging (Flexible and Rigid) industry (i.e. B2B market) and consumer goods market (i.e. B2B2C market).
1. Bio-plastics 100% based on agriculture by products
2. 100% bio-degradable and compostable within 15-30 days
3. 100% recyclable
4. Bio-plastic grades composed of 0-10% oil based polymer.
5. Non-hazardous to nature and all living beings
The utilization of potential agro-waste will results in new income opportunity for farmers and BoP population by consumption of unutilized agro-waste.
