Market Ready Product ‚Äî Haemadhaar Tablets

Anemia, Hematinic, Immunity booster, Blood purifier, Hepatoprotective, Anti-Oxidant & Anti-Sickling (Sickle Cell Anemia)

Research and Development

Lead: Prof. (Dr) Pooja J Doshi & Team, SPPU

  • Screening of a large number of medicinal plants for anti-sickling properties.
  • RBC & hemoglobin molecular-level characterization using advanced analytical techniques.
  • Efficacy & formulation development.
  • Toxicological & safety studies using animal models.

Formulation Contents

1. Aegle marmelos (Bael leaves)

Helps increase haemoglobin level, protects against infections, improves liver function & digestion.

2. Rubia cardifolia (Manjishtha root)

Effective blood purifier, improves immunity, liver & kidney function. Key component Alizarin identified as a potent anti-sickling agent.

3. Punica granatum (Pomegranate bark)

Excellent immunity enhancer, supports heart health; useful adjunct for cancer and diabetes management.

Post Marketing Clinical Surveillance ‚Äî Highlights

  • Improvement in haemoglobin levels.
  • Reduction in sickle cell crisis episodes and associated pain.
  • Conversion of sickled RBCs to normal-shaped RBCs (observed).
  • Improved liver function (SGPT/SGOT) and reduced bilirubin.
  • Improved kidney function and creatinine levels.
  • No reported side effects in monitored subjects.

NANO-HERBAL KAVACH

Hybrid disinfectant for extended protection of open surfaces

Research & Development: Krishna Vishwa Vidyapeeth (Karad) & DIC SPPU, Pune

Project Funded By: Design Innovation Centre, SPPU, Pune

Manufactured By: Nano Research Lab, Gopalpur, Jharkhand, India-832102

Product Features

  • Nano-herbal formulation with antibacterial, antifungal and antiviral properties.
  • Eco-friendly, non-toxic and aromatic.
  • Special applicator ‚ÄúCleaning Pod‚Äù for effective sanitization of stair grills and other surfaces.

Description

Frequent touch surfaces in public places get re-contaminated quickly. Conventional wiping removes germs only once. Nano-Herbal Kavach provides a thin, adherent coating that extends surface protection. The Cleaning Pod (adjustable case + sponge saturated with formulation) enables fast, precise, low-manpower sanitization of grills, escalator rails, trolley handles, etc. Advantages include reduced manpower, improved efficiency, easy handling and higher precision.

Advantages

  1. Thin coating can be applied over any surface.
  2. Nano materials create one-dimensional coating and strong surface adhesion.
  3. Customizable formulation for target use-cases.
  4. Recommended for public places, hospitals, travel hubs and sanitation workers.
  5. Broad-spectrum antimicrobial performance.
  6. Pleasant herbal aroma.

BASCIMIX

Research & Development: Ajinkya Terkar & Dr. Mahesh Y. Borde ‚Äî Design Innovation Centre, SPPU

Introduction: Endospore-forming bacilli (PGPR) are robust, stable bioinoculants that promote plant growth and biological control. Bascimix harnesses these advantages to improve soil & crop performance.

Advantages

  • Mobilizes insoluble potash.
  • Improves soil properties.
  • Increases brix level and fruit quality.
  • Increases yield by ~20% (field-dependent).
  • Enhances water uptake and root development.

Target Crops

  • Jowar
  • Bajra
  • Wheat
  • Maize
  • Paddy
  • Cotton
  • Sugarcane
  • Vegetables
