Haemadhaar Tablets

Indications: Anemia, Hematinic, Immunity booster, Blood purifier, Hepatoprotective, Anti-oxidant & Anti-sickling (Sickle Cell Anemia).

Research & Development

Lead: Prof. (Dr) Pooja J. Doshi & Team, SPPU

  • Screening of large number of medicinal plants for anti-sickling properties.
  • RBC & hemoglobin molecular-level characterization using advanced analytical techniques.
  • Efficacy & formulation development.
  • Toxicological & safety studies using animal models.

Formulation Contents

1. Aegle marmelos (Bael leaves)

Helps increase hemoglobin level; protective against infections; improves liver function & digestion.

2. Rubia cardifolia (Manjishtha root)

Blood purifier; improves immunity, liver & kidney function. Key component Alizarin identified as a potent anti-sickling agent.

3. Punica granatum (Pomegranate bark)

Immunity enhancer; supports heart function; useful adjunct in cancer & diabetes contexts.

Post-Marketing Clinical Surveillance ‚Äî Highlights

  • Improvement in hemoglobin levels.
  • Reduction in sickle cell crises and associated pain.
  • Conversion of sickled RBCs to normal RBCs.
  • Improved liver function (SGPT/SGOT) and reduced bilirubin.
  • Improved kidney function and creatinine levels.
  • No reported side effects in monitored subjects.

Nano-Herbal Kavach

Hybrid disinfectant for extended protection of open surfaces

R&D: Krishna Vishwa Vidyapeeth, Karad & DIC SPPU, Pune

Funded by: Design Innovation Centre, SPPU, Pune

Manufactured by: Nano Research Lab, Gopalpur, Jharkhand

Product Features

  • Nano-herbal formulation with antibacterial, antifungal and antiviral properties.
  • Eco-friendly, non-toxic and aromatic.
  • Special applicator ‚ÄúCleaning Pod‚Äù for effective sanitization of grills and other surfaces.

Description

Conventional surface sanitization removes germs once but does not keep surfaces protected. The Cleaning Pod (adjustable case + sponge saturated with nano-herbal formulation) enables quick, precise, low-manpower sanitization and leaves a thin adherent coating for extended protection on frequently touched public surfaces (rails, handles, trolleys).

Advantages

  1. Thin coating can be applied on any surface.
  2. Nano materials provide strong surface adhesion.
  3. Customizable formulation per use-case.
  4. Recommended for public places, hospitals, travel hubs and sanitation workers.
  5. Broad-spectrum antimicrobial activity.
  6. Pleasant herbal aroma.

BASCIMIX

R&D: Ajinkya Terkar & Dr. Mahesh Y. Borde ‚Äî Design Innovation Centre, SPPU

Introduction: PGPR bacilli (endospore-forming) are stable bioinoculants that promote plant growth and biological control. Bascimix leverages these traits for improved soil & crop performance.

Advantages

  • Mobilizes insoluble potash.
  • Improves soil properties.
  • Increases brix level and fruit quality.
  • Increases yield by ~20% (field dependent).
  • Improves water uptake and root development.

Target Crops

  • Jowar
  • Bajra
  • Wheat
  • Maize
  • Paddy
  • Cotton
  • Sugarcane
  • Vegetables
