Selected prototype & research projects developed under DIC SPPU

SOLAR SHUTTLE

Developed by: Arun A. Aynodkar, Prajkta R. Jape & Dr. A. D. Shaligram

Primary development of a solar shuttle for use within Savitribai Phule Pune University campus â€” a pollution-free, battery-operated electric vehicle with on-board solar charging (300W panel). A round trip ~3.6 km across campus, range ~50 km per charge. Six-plus-one seating. A 1.2 kW solar charging docking station and two spare battery sets are provided for quick battery swaps to maintain service quality.

SOLAR CHARGING STATION

Developed by: Arun A. Aynodkar, Prajkta R. Jape & Dr. A. D. Shaligram

Stationary solar charging station for Li-Ion batteries used by solar shuttle & solar bicycle. Solar panels on the roof charge battery sets during daytime; the station also serves as parking and enables rapid battery replacement when vehicles discharge. Designed to charge spare battery sets and improve service availability.

TABLETOP HYDROPONICS PLANTER

Developed by: Arun A. Aynodkar & Dr. A. D. Shaligram

An IoT-enabled tabletop hydroponics system for indoor gardening. Soil-free, nutrient-circulation design with oxygenation and automated controls. Compact & aesthetic form factor for desks/countertops; conserves water and accelerates plant growth.

ELECTRIC PURAN YANTRA

Developed by: Arun A. Aynodkar, Prajkta R. Jape & Dr. A. D. Shaligram

A kitchen appliance to grind & mix cooked chana dal with sugar and other ingredients for puran poli. Stainless steel blades, heat-resistant housing, overload protection, detachable parts for easy cleaning, and user-friendly controls for consistent texture.

CROP CUTTING MACHINE

Developed by: Deepak Gilgile & Dr. A. D. Shaligram

A manually operated, low-cost crop cutter for crops up to ~2 ft height. Uses cycle/bike parts and gear combinations to convert human pedaling into high-speed blade rotation. Features: hand operation, low maintenance, fuel-free, easy spare-part availability.

  • Hand operation, eco-friendly
  • Reduces human effort & cutting time
  • Low-cost maintenance

GREEN GOLD

Developed by: Jayashri Khadilkar & Dr. M. V. Kulkarni

"Green Gold" is a charcoal-based mycorrhizal biofertilizer (Glomus mosseae) that forms root networks to improve nutrient availability, increase yields and help plants endure abiotic stress. Suitable for many plant types (leafy greens, fruit trees, ornamentals).

TRICHO-CURE

Developed by: Tejal Rikame & Dr. Mahesh Y. Borde

A biocontrol product based on Trichoderma spp. isolates that inhibit soil-borne fungal pathogens (45â€“55% inhibition in vitro). Formulated in a talc-based carrier; improves plant growth and protects crops via competition, antibiosis and mycoparasitism.

PHOSPHATE SOLUBILIZING BACTERIA (PSB - ADVANCED)

Developed by: Tejal Rikame & Dr. Mahesh Y. Borde

PSB-Advanced converts insoluble soil P compounds into soluble forms (PSI ~2.14) and shows plant-growth promoting traits: IAA production, zinc solubilization, ammonia production, nitrogen fixation and biocontrol via HCN and antifungal metabolites. Identified as Pseudomonas spp.; suitable as a cost-effective biofertilizer.

STRESS FIGHTER

Developed by: Dr. Swapnil Mahajan & Dr. Mohan V. Kulkarni

A PGPR biofertilizer derived from indigenous stress-tolerant bacteria. Improves nutrient uptake, soil health and crop tolerance to salinity, drought and temperature stress. Demonstrated in greenhouse pot trials; field trials & certifications pending.

CHELATOR PLUS

Developed by: Dr. Swapnil Mahajan & Dr. Mohan V. Kulkarni

An eco-friendly chelator peptide formulation that improves iron & micronutrient availability to plants, has antimicrobial activity, and helps maintain nutritional quality. Pot trials for iron-limited conditions completed; field trials and certification pending.
